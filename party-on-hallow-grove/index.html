Description

Party on Hallow Grove is a small, cute halloween adventure game where you are tasked with throwing Death the best surprise birthday party EVER!

Talk to kooky guests, explore the mansion, trade party items, put up decorations, and play some party music - all before Death wakes up from his death nap.

PoHG plays as a top-down rpg with an emphasis on story, exploration, interaction, dialogue and item trading.

On your quest, you will laugh, make weird friends, and learn to be kind to one another - and when the party is crashed by an unexpected guest, youâ€™ll be faced with your biggest challenge yet.

History

Vaaasm began making Party on Hallow Grove in September of 2024 as a celebration of the fall and halloween season.
It is their debut game - with all programming, art, design, and music created by them.

Features

  • Explore Death's mansion and talk to a wide cast of spooky characters'
  • Complete quests as you trade and find party items
  • Clean and decorate the mansion before the party
  • Choose what party music to play as you get the AUX and unlock the OST
