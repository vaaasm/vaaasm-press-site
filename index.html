Description

vaaasm is the moniker of Nattie Torres Resto, a solo indie game developer based in Chicago, IL.

History

What began as nothing more than playing around with Game Maker in middle school, game-making soon turned into Nattie's' lifelong passion.

As vaaasm, Nattie aims to create distinctive indie games that evoke emotional investment in the player, with a strong focus on character-based stories, unique gameplay, and quality aesthetics.
